General Info.

The ARTRADS Dance Crew is part of the Performing Arts Group under the Socio-Cultural and Sports Development Unit at CvSU-CCAT.

The Socio-Cultural and Sports Development Unit, which oversees the ARTRADS Dance Crew, is supervised by a Coordinator for Socio-Cultural and Sports Development designated by the Campus Administrator.

ARTRADS aims to identify and support talented student dancers, helping them develop their skills and pursue their passion for dance. The organization is especially beneficial for those aspiring to be part of a dance group and achieve their dance-related goals.

Supporting ARTRADS in various events is immensely helpful to the organization. Cheering for them during their intermission dances and outside competitions provides great encouragement and is greatly appreciated.
